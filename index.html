Our fiscal year (April 2012 - June 2013) just ended here at Bread for the City. Staff and funders are often interested in how many people we served, how often, and where they are coming from. This map breaks down our services from the past year by DC neighborhoods.

We offer food and clothing to low-income DC residents, and within medical, social, and legal programs, we offer a number of services (click on a service area to see those breakdowns).

Of course, this map does not capture everything. We sometimes serve people from outside DC and nearly 20% of our visits are from people who are homeless or without stable housing. There are also countless support groups, training sessions, events, and everdyay interactions that make up our work at Bread for the City that we do not track. This is just one way of showing the work we do.
